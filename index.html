📋 How to Find Your Salesforce User ID

  1. Log in to Salesforce
  2. Click your avatar / profile picture in the top-right corner
  3. Click "Settings" from the dropdown
  4. In the URL bar you'll see something like:
    lightning/settings/personal/PersonalInformation/home
    — scroll down the page to "Advanced User Details"
  5. Your Salesforce User ID starts with 005 and is 18 characters long
    (e.g. 00521000003XXXXAAA)
Alternative: Ask your Salesforce admin or find it in
Setup → Users → click your name → the ID appears in the URL.

🐙 GitHub Setup Guide

Follow these steps to create your free GitHub account and connect it to Calebrate.

Part 1 — Create a GitHub Account
  1. Go to github.com/signup and click "Sign up"
  2. Enter your @gusto.com work email and choose a password
  3. Pick a username — something like FirstLastGusto
  4. Complete the verification steps and check your Gusto inbox to confirm your email
⚠️ Important: Do NOT click "Continue with Google" or any fast-sign-in shortcut. Those link to your personal Gmail. You must sign up with your @gusto.com email directly so your work data stays separate.
Part 2 — Create a Personal Access Token
  1. Once signed in, click your profile picture in the top-right corner → Settings
  2. Scroll all the way to the bottom of the left sidebar and click "Developer settings"
  3. Click "Personal access tokens" → "Tokens (classic)"
  4. Click "Generate new token" → "Generate new token (classic)"
  5. Give it a name like Calebrate Tracker, set expiration to "No expiration"
  6. Check the box next to "repo" (it says "Full control of private repositories") — that's the only one you need
  7. Scroll to the bottom and click the green "Generate token" button
  8. ⚠️ Copy your token immediately — it starts with ghp_ and you won't be able to see it again once you leave the page
💡 Tip: Paste the token somewhere safe (like a note) before coming back here. Once copied, paste it into the "Personal Access Token" field in this form.
🚀 That's it! You do NOT need to create a repo yourself. When you click "Connect & Launch Tracker," Calebrate will automatically create a private Prospect-tracker repo in your account and set everything up for you.
