Finding Your Salesforce User ID

  1. Log in to Salesforce
  2. Click your profile icon (top right) and select Settings
  3. In the left sidebar, click Personal Information
  4. Look for User ID — it starts with 005 and is 15-18 characters
  5. Copy and paste it here
